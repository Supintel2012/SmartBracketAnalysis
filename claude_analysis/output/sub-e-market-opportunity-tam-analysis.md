## 5. Competitive Market Analysis

### Market Maturity: **Emerging -> Consolidating**

AI bracket prediction went from novelty (2023) to commodity (2025).

### Competitor Landscape

| Competitor | Type | Price | Threat |
|------------|------|-------|--------|
| ESPN "Smart Bracket" | Incumbent | Free | High |
| ChatGPT/Claude/Grok | GenAI | Free/$20 | High |
| CBS/Yahoo Smart Pick | Incumbent | Free | Medium |
| Bracketology sites | Specialty | Free/Freemium | Medium |

### 2025 AI Performance Data (Critical Insight)

```
Claude Sonnet 3.7: 98.4th percentile (single year)
SmartBracket: 92.4th percentile (single year)

BUT:
SmartBracket cohort average: 80.9%
GenAI cohort average: 71.8%
SmartBracket 9-year average: ~90th percentile
```

**Key Insight**: GenAI has high variance, lucky in 2025. SmartBracket has lower variance, consistent performance. Core value = **consistent, sustainable edge** vs. **lottery ticket performance**.

### Incumbent Risk: **HIGH**
- ESPN already offers free "Smart Bracket"
- Sports betting platforms building proprietary analytics
- Major tech companies can replicate statistical models easily

### Margin Structure
**Race to bottom** for consumer bracket prediction. Free is the prevailing price.
